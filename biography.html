Hello!! My name is Eren (Ella) Adamiak

As you can probably tell this website is still a work in progress, I will continue to add more interesting things to it as I learn more about web design and coding. I am a junior graphic designer who also wants to do website design (HTML and CSS) . I am based in the New England area, striving to try and work in Kyoto/Tokyo or Osaka, Japan. I focus on logo/branding design and website design, wanting to illustrate more. The type of work that I tend to gravitate toward is website design and branding for companies. Other creative ideas that I love to design around are bold designs with a lot of colors and interesting fonts, willing to work in all other design types as well. I approach design with multiple mock ups making sure I have original designs so I can reference back to them and I have the original if I want to go back to it. The values I always strive for is collaboration on the project itself, no matter what it is I would always love feedback and constructive criticism so I can improve on the design. I always want a lot of my pieces to be full of character, geared to the client's wants and needs that they want to see in their branding/logo/website. Inclusive design for everyone, as well as collaboration with other mediums of design. What inspires me outside of a design aspect is japanese culture, fashion such as streetwear, as well as the 90's and grunge aesthetic. I am always looking for new things to learn and try out, so if you have any suggestions please let me know! Some other things that a geared towards non-design aspects of my life is that I am a very active K-pop dancer, I am also a part of a K-pop dance group called "K-Pop Dance Crew" where we do covers of songs and perform at events.
